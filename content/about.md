# About

I design for complexity — where business systems, human workflows, and organizational reality collide. I go to where the work happens (warehouses, retail floors, call centers), observe real users under real constraints, and translate those observations into pragmatic product decisions.

Design philosophy
- Solve high-stakes, enterprise problems where clarity and execution move the needle.
- Field research first: evidence beats assumption.
- Ship pragmatic solutions: prioritize what helps users reach value quickly.

What energizes me
- Enterprise and B2B problems with real operational constraints.
- Projects where field research shifts direction and clarifies opportunity.
- Building AI products grounded in user need, not trend-chasing.

Roles I'm targeting: Senior Product Designer, Staff Product Designer, Principal UX Designer. Remote-first or Utah-based preferred.
